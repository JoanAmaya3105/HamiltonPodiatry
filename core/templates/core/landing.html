About Your Podiatrist

Anna-Gloria Biladi is a qualified podiatrist who is passionate about helping people feel comfortable, mobile, and confident on their feet.

She works with patients of all ages — from those needing routine care to active individuals dealing with sports-related concerns. Anna-Gloria takes the time to understand your needs, providing care that is gentle, personalised, and focused on long-term results.

🩺 How We Can Help expand_more

We treat a wide range of foot and lower limb concerns, including:
- General foot care (nail trimming, corns and calluses)
- Ingrown toenails
- Foot pain and discomfort
- Diabetic foot care
- Fungal nail conditions
- Ongoing foot maintenance

💪 Sports & Activity Care expand_more

If you're active or play sports, your feet go through a lot of stress. We provide care for common activity-related issues such as:

- Heel pain
- Arch pain
- Overuse injuries
- Running-related foot pain
- Gym and training-related discomfort

Our focus is not just on treatment, but also on prevention and performance, helping you recover properly and get back to doing what you enjoy with confidence.
